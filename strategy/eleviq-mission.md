# ElevIQ Capability Alignment System

## Overview

ElevIQ is a capability alignment system that helps individuals discover the connection between their natural strengths, learning pathways, and meaningful work.

Instead of traditional assessments, ElevIQ focuses on alignment between people, learning, and opportunity.

The goal is to help individuals move toward purpose-driven careers.

---

## Organizational Structure

ElevIQ operates through two organizations.

ElevIQ Foundation
A nonprofit organization focused on expanding access to capability alignment tools for students, veterans, and underserved communities.

STC Innovations
A deployment and implementation partner that helps schools, workforce programs, and organizations use the ElevIQ system.

---

## Technology Platform

The ElevIQ system runs on a white-labeled AI platform developed by MayaMaya.

STC Innovations developed the alignment framework, use cases, and deployment model that sit on top of the platform.

---

## How the System Works

Participants complete a capability alignment scan.

The system analyzes responses and identifies:

• natural strengths
• potential learning pathways
• potential career alignment opportunities

Participants receive a report and guidance that helps them move toward meaningful work and continued learning.
