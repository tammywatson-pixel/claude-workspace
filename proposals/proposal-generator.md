# ElevIQ Proposal Generator

This document provides instructions for generating partnership proposals, pilot proposals, and grant narratives related to the ElevIQ capability alignment system.

Claude should reference the file strategy/eleviq-mission.md when generating any proposal.

---

## Proposal Structure

When creating proposals, organize them in the following format.

### 1. Introduction

Introduce ElevIQ as a capability alignment system that helps individuals discover the connection between their natural strengths, learning pathways, and meaningful work.

Explain that the system focuses on alignment rather than traditional assessment models.

---

### 2. The Problem

Describe common challenges faced by students, workforce participants, or career changers:

• difficulty identifying strengths
• lack of alignment between education and careers
• unclear learning pathways
• workforce transitions

---

### 3. The ElevIQ Solution

Explain how ElevIQ addresses these issues through capability alignment.

The system helps individuals:

• identify natural strengths
• explore learning pathways
• connect strengths with career opportunities

---

### 4. Pilot Program Structure

When proposing a pilot, include:

• number of participants
• timeline
• participant experience
• advisor or educator involvement

---

### 5. Expected Outcomes

Examples:

• improved career clarity for participants
• stronger alignment between education and workforce pathways
• better advising conversations
• improved engagement in learning pathways

---

### 6. Next Steps

Include clear next actions such as:

• scheduling a demonstration
• launching a pilot cohort
• exploring grant funding
