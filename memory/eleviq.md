## What Is ElevIQ?

ElevIQ is a **capability alignment system** that helps individuals discover the
intersection between their natural strengths, learning pathways, and meaningful
work. Rather than relying on traditional assessments, ElevIQ emphasizes
self-understanding first — capabilities, motivations, and potential — and then
connects those insights to education, workforce, and personal growth pathways.

---

## Mission

> Help people understand themselves first, then connect those insights to
> education pathways, workforce opportunities, and personal growth.

ElevIQ guides users through career exploration, educational decisions, and
workforce development using a **capability alignment scan** that generates
insights on how individuals think, collaborate, solve problems, and learn.

---

## Organizational Structure

ElevIQ operates through two connected organizations:

### ElevIQ Foundation _(Nonprofit)_
- Expands access to capability alignment for students, veterans, career
  changers, and underserved communities
- Partners with schools, workforce programs, and community organizations
- Primary vehicle for grant funding and mission-driven access programs

### STC Innovations _(For-Profit)_
- Implementation partner that deploys the ElevIQ capability alignment system
- Serves universities, workforce boards, and companies
- Develops the ElevIQ framework, alignment methodology, and use cases
- Licenses and white-labels the **MayaMaya** technology platform

## Key Differentiators

- **Alignment over assessment** — not a personality test or skills inventory;
  focuses on capability patterns and meaningful work fit
- **Dual-structure model** — nonprofit foundation for access + for-profit for
  enterprise deployment
- **Methodology ownership** — ElevIQ controls the framework and use cases
  even though the platform is white-labeled
- **Purpose-driven framing** — centers the individual's journey toward
  meaningful, purpose-driven careers
