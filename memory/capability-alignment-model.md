# ElevIQ Capability Alignment Model

_Last updated: 2026-03-13_

ElevIQ is a capability alignment system that helps individuals discover the
connection between their natural strengths, learning pathways, and meaningful work.

The system is designed to move beyond traditional testing or assessment models
and instead focuses on alignment between the individual and real-world opportunities.

---

## Core Concept

Capability alignment means helping a person understand how their natural tendencies,
motivations, and interests intersect with real learning pathways and workforce opportunities.

> The goal is not to label individuals but to guide them toward environments where they can thrive.

---

## Key Components

### 1. Capability Alignment Scan

The system begins with a short capability alignment scan that identifies patterns
in how individuals think, work, and engage with problems.

The scan focuses on:

- **Curiosity and exploration** — how a person approaches new ideas and information
- **Problem solving approach** — how they think through challenges
- **Collaboration tendencies** — how they work with others
- **Learning preferences** — how they absorb and apply knowledge
- **Motivation drivers** — what energizes and sustains their engagement

This produces a high-level **capability profile** for the individual.

---

### 2. Alignment Insights

After the scan, individuals receive a report highlighting patterns in their
capabilities and potential directions for exploration.

- Focus is on **possibilities rather than rigid classifications**
- Designed to encourage reflection and discussion with advisors, mentors, or educators
- Framed as a starting point for exploration, not a final verdict

---

### 3. Learning Pathways

The system connects capability patterns with concrete learning pathways, which may include:

- Academic programs
- Technical certifications
- Workforce training
- Experiential learning

> The goal is to show multiple routes toward meaningful work.

---

### 4. Advisor Console

Advisors and educators can review participant insights in a dashboard that supports conversations about:

- Career exploration
- Skill development
- Learning options
- Next steps

This makes ElevIQ a tool for guided dialogue — not just self-guided discovery.

---

### 5. Talent Intelligence Layer

Over time, the system analyzes capability patterns across groups of participants,
allowing organizations to identify:

- Capability clusters within a population
- Training and skill gaps
- Alignment (or misalignment) between education programs and workforce roles

This layer creates actionable insight for schools, workforce organizations, and employers.

---

## Target Populations

ElevIQ is designed to serve:

| Population | Context |
|------------|---------|
| Students | Exploring career direction before or during education |
| Veterans | Transitioning into civilian careers |
| Workforce reskilling participants | Career changers seeking new pathways |
| Early career professionals | Seeking purposeful direction after entering the workforce |
| Underserved communities | Populations with limited access to career guidance |
